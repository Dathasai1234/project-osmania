About Fest

"TechnoSmania 2023" is an occasion where all techies are given an opportunity to flaunt their technical expertise with events such as Debates, paper presentations, poster presentation, Quizzes, Industry defined Problem, Technical Project Contest & artistic finesse with events like Catalysis, Texchroma, Synergism, Technopharma, Singing. Dancing, Short film making. Photography, Skit and Painting competitions. We also have special attractions at Technosmania 2023 in the form Of multiple musical and cultural events, Conceived long ago, the annual Techno-CuIturaI fest of four departments of the college, TechnoSmania 2023 has become bigger, better and even more competitive. It's a melting pot for novices from different backgrounds and cultures from all over the nation & indeed a chance for everyone to experience ecstasy with a delight fulfillment. This time, TechnoSmania 2023 promises to create a colloquium of all sorts showcasing a surprisingly impressive and notable panorama and alchemy.

Events

ANTHARANG

Antharang is an endeavour to discover the creative side of all our techies Antharang constitutes a plethora of literary & cultural events that are creative, fun filled and exciting. A great place to exhibit all the talent and win exciting prizes. Antharang includes events like Singing, Dancing, Short film making, Photography, Skit , Painting competitions and much more.

FOOD SYNERGISM

Food, the "Prime Objective Of the Day". The major strength of Food Technology is the Synergism that exists among the students, industry and research institutions. This fest offers ecstasy through a plethora of events, aims to emphasize on the newly , emerging trends, motivating students and exhibit their potential to enrich their knowledge and prepare them for future needs of the Society.

TEXCHROMA

The primary objective is to create an awareness about the role & application of textiles in various fields from household to Aerospace. Tex Chroma is with a logo "Clothing the Nation" & "Dress is index of Human " hidden talent in textile field through identification & appreciation. Tex Chroma is a spectrum of events with paper & poster presentations, project, to enrich the knowledge.

TECHNOPHARMA

A Technopharma Symposium with an objective of bringing you on a journey of presentations by well versed experts in their respective fields & also to share and exchange ideas on current situations and latest advancements in the pharmaceutical world. Events like paper & poster presentations, technical quiz etc. scintillating attractions will be held to encourage students to think out of the box & widen their in the field of research.

CATALYSIS

A technical symposium with an objective of bringing about the best engineers in the country in the domain of chemical engineering to dwell into the latest advancements in the field of chemical and process engineering with loads of events like paper presentations, poster presentations, technical quiz and scintillating attractions.
